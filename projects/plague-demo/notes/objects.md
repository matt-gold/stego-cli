# Objects

## OBJ-ASTROLABE

- Name: Brass astrolabe
- Description: Instrument used for altitude and hour in astronomical calculation
- Symbolism: Cosmic order sought under civic collapse

## OBJ-ALMANAC

- Name: Arabic-Latin almanac
- Description: Tables for conjunctions, aspects, and eclipses used in medical astrology
- Symbolism: Inherited authority under strain

## OBJ-LEAD-SEAL

- Name: Lead talisman seal of Saturn and Jupiter
- Description: Protective image made at an elected hour according to astral image-magic practice
- Symbolism: Open secret at the boundary of orthodoxy

## OBJ-GRAIN-SACK

- Name: Torn grain sack from the quay
- Description: Sackcloth from riverside storage, marked by heavy handling and contamination
- Symbolism: Material life as secondary cause

## OBJ-FORBIDDEN-QUIRE

- Name: Unbound quire from the Picatrix tradition
- Description: Partial Latin copy of astral image instructions kept out of formal inventory
- Symbolism: Illicit method in search of lawful explanation
