# Locations

## LOC-COLLEGE

- Name: College chamber near the Rue Saint-Jacques
- Type: Study and lecture room
- Historical context: University physicians framed plague causes through disputation and commentary

## LOC-HOTELDIEU

- Name: Hotel-Dieu ward
- Type: Crowded hospital near Notre-Dame
- Historical context: Clergy, surgeons, and lay caregivers worked in overlapping religious and medical practice

## LOC-QUAY

- Name: Grain quay on the Seine
- Type: River unloading and transfer zone
- Historical context: Food, cloth, and animals moved through dense commercial contact

## LOC-CHARNEL

- Name: Charnel precinct at cemetery edge
- Type: Overflow burial ground
- Historical context: Mortality surges compressed ritual time and civic capacity
