# Characters

## CHAR-MATTHAEUS

- Name: Magister Matthaeus de Rota
- Role: Scholar of medicine and astrology at the University of Paris
- Commitments: Scholastic causality, humoral medicine, and celestial influence
- Open secret: Consults suspect texts of astral magic for practical remedies

## CHAR-ETIENNE

- Name: Etienne of Saint-Marcel
- Role: Young cleric-scribe attached to Matthaeus
- Commitments: Careful notation, doctrinal caution, and loyalty to method

## CHAR-AGNES

- Name: Agnes the apothecary
- Role: Compounder and supplier near the Petit Pont
- Commitments: Practical observation, household medicine, and blunt speech

## CHAR-RAOUL

- Name: Canon Raoul de Villiers
- Role: Cathedral canon and royal intermediary overseeing the inquiry
- Commitments: Public order, orthodoxy, and language fit for governance
